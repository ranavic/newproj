Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and profile details. We also collect information about your learning preferences, course progress, and interactions with the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Provide and improve our services
  • Communicate with you about your account and updates
  • Generate analytics and reports on user behavior (in aggregate)
  • Create personalized AI-driven recommendations for courses and content

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, gather general visitor information, and track visits to our website. We use this information to improve our website and better understand our users' needs.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to help us operate our platform or administer activities on our behalf. These third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your information for any other purpose.

6. AI Learning Data

Our AI Tutor and personalized learning features collect data about your learning patterns and preferences. This data is used to improve your learning experience and is processed in accordance with our commitment to privacy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of certain data collection features. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our support channels.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@skillforge.edu.
